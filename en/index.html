JOCELYN BELL The Voice of Pulsars

An educational project on the recognition of women in science, exploring the story of Jocelyn Bell Burnell and her revolutionary discovery of pulsars.

What is the Matilda Effect?

The Matilda Effect describes the denial or minimization of women scientists' contributions, whose work is often attributed to their male colleagues. Jocelyn Bell Burnell is a striking example.

Our site is committed to breaking the silence around the Matilda Effect: we refuse to let the work of women scientists be erased, ignored, or attributed to others.
